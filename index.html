ITALY

Walk through the pages of history - picturesque - romantic - trip to wonderland - Wow! The Pizzaa. - Beware of the rip off artists in Roman costume - Long Queues - Go for the pasta, not for the view - Mostly pricey - A don't miss for Art lovers!

NEW ZEALAND

Beautiful, even in the rain - Tekapo, A jewel in the crown - Scandalous - A Birdwatcher's paradise - Climb her, it's well worth the effort - The Shire comes to life - Bucketlist Hike.

NORWAY

Dramatic Scenery - Fairytale landscape - Unspoilt beauty - tough climb, but worth it - Great experience spoilt by crowd - Best public restroom view in the world!

PRAGUE

Czech this out! - Something for everyone - History Galore! - Touristic eye candy - Very busy - Go for the beer, stay for the company - Confusing tour guide - Fascinating, Engaging and Fun!

TURKEY

History at its best - Beyond out wildest dreams - Bought tears of Joy - Experience of a lifetime - White heaven - Tourist inferno - A joke of a tour :( - Pricey and Overrated.
NEPAL

Charming, historic and peaceful - Beautiful site with an amazing story - greatest trekking adventure of a lifetime - A gem for the 5 senses - MONKEYS! :/ - A nedival town, frozen in time - Sunrise spectacle - Challenging, Inspiring and Mesmerizing!
BRAZIL

Amazing waterfalls, but just busy - easy to see why it's rated world's best beach - beware of long lines - Iconic - Outstanding view, but watch out for pickpocketing - Christ and the mosquitoes :/ - swim with sharks and turtles!

MALDIVES

Moments for life - Just Go - Best wreck dive - Stunning Corals - Little heaven on Indian ocean - Best Honeymoon spot - 18 USD Plus tax for an Orange Juice?! :/

SWITZERLAND

Six star Wonderland - train to the top of the world - dragon tale - Chaotic and a rip off! :( - Top of the world - Lovely lake in a timeless setting - Definitely a place of angels - Watch it until your eyes bleed!!
GREECE

Journey Back in Time - Worth the 300 steps - The Marbles shine like the sun - Building site :/ - Ruins on Strike! - Cocktail with a view - Oia - Fira is AMAZING! - Gem in the wild.

CHINA

Truly Spectacular view - Great Wall Winner! - Magical - The Best Dumplings ever!! - Crowd crown crowd! - Too Touristy - Just can't get enough.

TANZANIA

Climb kili - Almost a jurassic park - amazing safari in the biggest zoo in the world - Add this to your list, even if you don't have one! - have fun kitesurfing - Gorgeous Sandbanks!
